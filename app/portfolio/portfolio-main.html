Design Process

My design process attempts to combine the principles of Design Thinking with a respect of and understanding for the development process. Typically, when a design request is received, I will attempt to understand as much as possible about what the function of the site or page will be. If there is an existing page or portal, I will complete a Heuristic Evaluation, as well as interview current users of the system. If not, I will interview potential users, as well as team members of the business making the request in an effort to Empathize with the User. This step also involves conducting user interviews and understanding user feedback to define personas. Next, I will Define the Problem Statement in terms of the defined users. I will then work on low-fidelity Prototyping in order to facilitate quick User Testing. I will then Build a High-Fidelity Prototype utilizing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
